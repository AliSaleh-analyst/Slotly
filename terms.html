5. App Features

Joker Card: Each habit earns one Joker Card every 10 days. A Joker Card can be used the day after a missed habit to retroactively mark it as complete and preserve your streak. Joker Cards are non-transferable between habits and expire if not used within one day of becoming applicable.

Home Screen Widget: Slotly offers an optional home screen widget that displays up to 5 of your habits and their completion status for the current day. Widget data is read from local storage only.

CSV Export: You can export your full habit history as a CSV file from Settings. The export is generated locally and shared only through the Android share sheet at your direction.

Badges: The app awards achievement badges for milestones such as completing habits on consecutive days. Badges are stored locally on your device.
