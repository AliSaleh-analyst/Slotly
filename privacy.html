Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Slotly: Habit Tracker, developed and operated by Ali Saleh ("we", "our", or "the app"), collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Email address — if you create an account using email/password or sign in with Google, we collect your email address.
  • Your name — if you choose to enter a display name in app settings. This is optional and stored only on your device.

Information stored on your device

  • Your habits, completion history, streaks, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not have access to your habit data. It never leaves your device unless you sign in to sync.

Information stored in the cloud (optional)

  • If you create an account, we store your Pro subscription status in Google Firebase Firestore, linked to your user ID. This allows you to restore Pro access on a new device.
  • We do not store your habits or personal data in the cloud.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and restore your Pro subscription across devices.
  • To send habit reminder notifications (only if you enable them).
  • To process subscription payments via Google Play Billing.
  • To generate personalised AI coaching responses via Google Gemini (only when you use the Coach tab).

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with any third parties except as described below.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase Authentication — handles account login (email/password and Google Sign-In). Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Cloud Firestore — stores your Pro subscription status. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — processes all in-app purchases. We do not store or see your payment details. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini API (AI Coach feature) — when you use the AI Coach, your habit names and today's completion status are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate personalised coaching responses. This data is processed by Google according to their Gemini API Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy. Slotly does not store your AI Coach conversations. You can use the app without the AI Coach — it does not activate unless you navigate to the Coach tab.

4. Account Creation (Optional)

Creating an account is completely optional. The app works fully without an account. An account is only needed if you want to restore your Pro subscription on a new device.

5. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Cloud Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your email and Pro status from our servers. Your local habit data on your device is not affected.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data (email, Pro status) is retained until you delete your account.
  • Local habit data remains on your device until you uninstall the app.

7. Children's Privacy

Slotly is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

8. Security

We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore, secured by Google's infrastructure. All data transmitted between the app and Firebase is encrypted using TLS.

9. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of Jordan. Any disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the courts of Jordan.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
